Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Wanderlust Tours different from other travel agencies? +

Wanderlust Tours stands out through our personalized approach, expert local guides, and commitment to creating authentic travel experiences. We don't just book trips; we craft unforgettable journeys tailored to your interests. Our team has firsthand experience in every destination we offer, ensuring we can provide genuine recommendations and insider knowledge.

Do you offer 24/7 support during my trip? +

Yes! Our dedicated support team is available 24/7 throughout your journey. Whether you need assistance with a reservation change, have questions about local customs, or encounter an emergency, we're just a phone call away. Each traveler receives a personalized emergency contact card with direct numbers to our local partners and headquarters team.

How do you select your accommodations? +

We personally vet every property we partner with, ensuring they meet our high standards for comfort, location, service, and authenticity. We prioritize unique, locally-owned properties that enhance your travel experience rather than generic chain hotels. Our team visits each property regularly to maintain quality standards and build strong relationships with owners.

Can you accommodate special dietary requirements? +

Absolutely! We understand that dietary needs are important. Please inform us of any allergies, restrictions, or preferences when booking, and we'll ensure all included meals and restaurant recommendations accommodate your requirements. Our local guides are also briefed on your needs to assist during your trip.
